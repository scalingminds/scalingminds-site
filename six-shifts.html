The Problem

Why Most Executive Teams Plateau

  • Real tension gets buried under surface-level agreement.
  • Accountability slips — or stays with one or two people.
  • Strategy execution is inconsistent and reactive.
  • Culture turns into noise instead of a strategic signal.

Not a Workshop

This Isn't a Workshop. It's a Leadership Operating System.

The Six Shifts™ function as a leadership operating system — centered around trust, candor, and alignment. At the core is true leadership. Around it: the behaviors that turn strategy into execution and culture into momentum.

This isn't a training. It's a transformation.

The Six Shifts™ help executives and their teams:

  • Stop avoiding hard conversations and start speaking the truth in the room
  • Replace vague roles and siloed decisions with shared accountability
  • Align around what matters most — and follow through
  • Empower more leaders to step up instead of relying on just one or two
  • Make leadership consistent, not reactive

When these six shifts take hold, the leadership team becomes an engine of clarity, trust, and execution. And that changes everything.

Is It Right For You?

Are The Six Shifts™ Right For Your Team?

The Six Shifts™ is built for executive teams who want to lead differently. It's for the group that holds strategic, cultural, and operational leadership across the business.

It's a fit if:

  • Your team is smart and capable — but struggles to have the honest conversations that drive clarity and progress
  • Accountability is inconsistent — some people follow through, others don't, and no one names it
  • You spend more time reacting to issues than driving strategy forward
  • Trust is mostly polite — but hard truths and real feedback don't get said in the room
  • A few leaders carry most of the weight, while others stay passive or disconnected
  • You're committed to growth — not just for the business, but for how you lead together

It's not a fit if:

  • You're looking for a one-off workshop or retreat to overhaul your leadership team
  • The leadership team isn't aligned on the need for change
  • You're not ready to name what's really happening — or shift how you lead
The Process

From Insight to Execution

The Six Shifts™ isn't a one-off event — it's a structured partnership designed to change how your team works together every day.

Get a clear view of your team's current leadership habits, dynamics, and alignment gaps.

Private coaching for senior leaders to support growth, confidence, and alignment.

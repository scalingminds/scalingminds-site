Meet Andy

Hi, I'm Andy

I spent years leading creative teams at one of Chicago's premier live theaters. Night after night, I watched what happened when talented people trusted each other enough to do their best work together and what happened when they didn't. That experience shaped everything about how I work with executive teams today.

I created The Six Shifts™ because most leadership teams have the talent, the strategy, and the ambition — but they don't have the trust, the candor, or the shared accountability to execute together. I work with CEOs and their leadership teams to close that gap. Not with a one-off workshop. With a system that changes how the team operates every day, in every meeting, on every decision.
